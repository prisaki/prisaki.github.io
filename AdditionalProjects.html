This is a list of older miscellaneous projects I have worked on, mostly completed during graduate school.

 

PEDL: Mobility as a Service

PEDL was a project in conjunction with Catapult Systems Transportation. Our brief tasked us with analyzing and identifying future trends and solutions for transportation services, with an emphasis on areas outside of major cities.

More

Frescho: Service Design and Social Innovation

Freshco is a concept to re-purpose an Old Rectory building in the Loughborough town center. This building originally was re-purposed as a museum for the English Midlands. However, there was a limited pool of volunteers to maintain the property, leading to a crisis of disuse and funding.

More

Major Project: Canopy Reading

Canopy Reading is an Augmented Reality (AR) application that connects readers and enhances the reading experience of physical books. This case study documents research and usability testing.
