Active Network

I was the primary researcher for 13 products at Active Network. I worked with diverse teams in the US and China across marketing, design, sales, and product management departments. My goal at Active was to reduce uncertainty and allow teams to enable decision-making, and advo-cate for our end-users. My projects ranged from exploratory research, usability studies, heuris-tic reviews, interviews, competitive analysis, and data visualizations.
